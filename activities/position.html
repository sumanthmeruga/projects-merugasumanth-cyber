What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

In CSS, different units of measurement are used to control the size and spacing of elements on a webpage. Common units include pixels (px) for fixed sizes, percentages (%) for relative sizes, em and rem for text-based scaling, and vh and vw for sizes based on the browser window.
