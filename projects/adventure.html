Adventure Activities & Safety

From hiking and surfing to wildlife spotting, adventure travel is rewarding but requires planning. Below are popular activities and safety tips.

Popular activities

Hiking, surfing, diving, and wildlife tours are common. Research trail difficulty, seasons, and certifications required for activities like diving.

Safety guidelines

Carry navigation, sufficient water, sun protection, and tell someone your route. For guided activities, check operator reviews and insurance coverage.

Personal stories

Short traveler accounts highlight preparation tips, mistakes to avoid, and how to find reputable local guides.
