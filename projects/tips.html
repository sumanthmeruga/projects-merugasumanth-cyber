Packing & Safety Tips

Practical advice to help you pack smarter, travel safer, and stretch your travel budget.

Packing tips

Rolling clothes saves space and reduces wrinkles. Use packing cubes to separate outfits and keep essentials accessible. Bring a compact first-aid kit and travel-size toiletries.

Safety for solo travelers

Share your itinerary with a trusted contact, avoid poorly lit areas at night, and keep copies of important documents in secure cloud storage. Learn a few local phrases to build rapport.

Budget travel advice

Travel off-season for lower prices, use public transport, eat where locals eat, and compare local guesthouses before booking. Track expenses using a simple notes app.

Cultural etiquette

Research dress codes, local gestures, and customary greetings. Respect local rules at religious sites and always ask before photographing people.
